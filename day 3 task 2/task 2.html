Nature is the physical world, including the laws, elements, and phenomena that govern it.
It can also refer to the living beings that inhabit the world, and the processes that govern them, such as the weather and geology.

Importance

Nature is essential for the survival of all living beings on Earth.
It provides the oxygen, sunlight, water, soil, and other resources that living things need.

Challenges

However, human activity can threaten nature.
Deforestation, pollution, and the exploitation of natural resources can all harm the environment.
